Bottle filler

Project has been started by an simple bottle filling machine for high school project as result of my lazyness to fill bottles at home.
Then i decided to level it up by idea with closing machine
I combined togehter two stations which resulted in this machine
Original concept was designed with flowmeter,but unfortonetly time was pushing me and flowmeter which we had in school wasn't working.
So i improvised and made it working by time which was linked with standart bottle volumes
Same issue with time occured with concept of automatic height adjust for all stations (filling,capping,closing)
Concept of automatic height adjust had to work by pneumatics cylinders with pressure regulator controlled by entered volume of the bottle u want to fill from PLC machine
This was then solved by manual height adjust by screws in pillar, by loosening the screws u could adjust height of the station by marks on each pillar with label of height.
